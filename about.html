About Us

Due to the recent pandemic, almost everything has changed. A routine appointment with a doctor could become a hassle for some, during a time when people needed reliable medical advice and help the most. We sought to solve this problem, and our solution is MedTech. MedTech provides affordable virtual scheduling and telecommunications between doctor's and their patients through mobile applications for scheduling, audio calls, video chats, and secure high-speed networks. Now more than ever, people need something they can rely on and trust, and that's what we hope to provide. Meet out team.
