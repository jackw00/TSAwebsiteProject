The Problem

The world was put on hold during the start of the coronavirus pandemic and has never fully resumed, yet people now more than ever needed medical attention and advice from doctor's they know they can trust. The safest way for doctor's to consult with patients was virtually, as some patients were unable or restricted from seeing a doctor in person. However, there was no one solution to this problem where doctor's had a simple communications company to help link them and their patients at a reasonable price.

We have created an affordable telecommunications service for your medical practice. Our provide the connection between doctor's and patients, 100 percent virtually. All of our packages bring you a scheduling website and mobile application that will allow patients to schedule appointments themselves virtually based on your availability. From your side, the scheduling website and mobile application will allow you to see when you have virtual appointments scheduled with patients. Built-in video chat and audio call services, along with 24/7 customer service for technology issues is available.
